How to Get Around

MBTA Subway Trains


MBTA Subway Trains (and buses) serves the Boston metropolitan area, colloquially referred to as “the T”. As the oldest transit subway in the nation still in use, the color-branded lines consist of the Red, Orange, Blue and Green Lines. One-way fares, daily passes and weekly passes are available for the subway and buses. Standard One-Way Fares are $2.40 for the subway or $1.70 for local buses. You may also purchase a 1-Day (24 hours) Pass for $12.75 or a 7-Day Pass for $22.50 which are valid on the subway and local buses. MBTA also operates ferry services to Charlestown from Long Wharf, the service is included in the 1-Day and 7-Day passes.

Bluebikes


Bluebikes is the bicycle sharing system in the Boston metropolitan area. The system has 325 stations and over 3500 bikes that serve the Boston, Cambridge, Everett, Somerville and Brookline areas. A single trip pass is $2.50 for every 30 minutes, ideal for one-way rides. An explorer 24-hour pass is also available for $10, ideal for riders looking to hit up several destinations in one day. You may purchase a pass through the kiosks located at each station or through the mobile app. Boston and Cambridge are considered one the most bike-friendly and best places for cyclists in the nation.

By foot


Boston ranked as the third most walkable large city in the country and has been identified as a “pedestrian’s perfect city”. All of the recommended attractions are walkable to and from each other and to and from downtown Boston. Even if you choose to not walk the entire trip to or from an attraction, a Bluebike station, a bus stop or a MBTA station will be within a short walk away.

Old Town Trolley Tours


Old Town Trolley Tours serves 18 convenient stops in Boston with frequent services. The ride passes include unlimited hop-on & hop-offs, as often as you like all day long. (Hop on/hop off feature is currently unavailable, it will be available again starting April 1, 2021). Their state-of-the-art stadium trolleys offers the best view, as you ride high above it all. The trolley tour is $43 per person that comes with tour guides provide 100% live tour narrations in real time.
